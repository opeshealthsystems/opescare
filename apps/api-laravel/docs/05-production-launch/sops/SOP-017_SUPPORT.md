# SOP-017 — Support & Helpdesk

**SOP Number:** SOP-017 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Support Desk Staff

---

## 1. Support Tiers

| Tier | Role | SLA |
|------|------|-----|
| L1 | Helpdesk agent | 4 business hours first response |
| L2 | Technical support | 1 business day |
| L3 | Engineering | As required |

## 2. Ticket Management
1. Accept via portal/email/phone/in-app. Create ticket in OpesCare Support module.
2. Triage: assign priority (Critical/High/Medium/Low) and tier. Send acknowledgement.
3. Investigate, document resolution steps, confirm with user, close ticket.

## 3. Escalation
- Critical not resolved in 2 hours: escalate to L2.
- L2 not resolved in 4 hours: escalate to L3 + management.

# SOP-005 — Appointments & Booking

**SOP Number:** SOP-005 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Receptionist / Appointments Clerk

---

## 1. Purpose
Standardise appointment booking, confirmation, reminders, and attendance tracking.

## 2. Booking Procedure
1. Verify patient (SOP-002).
2. Select date, time, provider, appointment type.
3. Check for scheduling conflicts.
4. Confirm with patient. System sends automated SMS/email confirmation.

## 3. Reminders
OpesCare auto-sends 24-hour and optional 2-hour reminders.

## 4. Attendance
- Patient arrives: check in to queue (SOP-006).
- No-show after 15 min: mark DNA (Did Not Attend).

## 5. Cancellations
- Patient-initiated: accept, free slot, send confirmation.
- Facility-initiated: notify patient min 24 hours in advance, offer reschedule.

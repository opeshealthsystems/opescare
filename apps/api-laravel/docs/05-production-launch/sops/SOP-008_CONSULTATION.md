# SOP-008 — Clinical Consultation

**SOP Number:** SOP-008 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Clinician

---

## 1. Pre-Consultation
Verify patient identity. Review history, allergies, active medications, and pending CDSS alerts.

## 2. Consultation Procedure

### SOAP Note
- **S** Subjective: chief complaint, history of presenting illness
- **O** Objective: examination findings, vital signs, investigations
- **A** Assessment: diagnosis / differential
- **P** Plan: investigations, prescriptions, referrals, follow-up

### CDSS Alert Review
Review all clinical decision support alerts.
**CDSS alerts are decision-support only — they do not replace clinical judgment.**
If overriding: document reason (min 10 chars) and category.

### Issue Orders
- Prescriptions: SOP-010
- Lab requests: SOP-009
- Referrals: referral workflow

### Close Visit
Mark consultation complete. Queue for pharmacy/lab as needed. Book follow-up if required.

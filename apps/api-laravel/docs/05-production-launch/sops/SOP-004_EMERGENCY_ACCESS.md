# SOP-004 — Emergency Access

**SOP Number:** SOP-004 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Emergency / Clinical Lead

---

## 1. Purpose
Define the procedure for accessing patient records without prior consent in a genuine medical emergency.

## 2. Criteria for Emergency Access
- Patient is unconscious and cannot consent
- Life is at immediate risk
- No authorised representative available in time
- Access is necessary for immediate care

## 3. Procedure

### Step 1 — Confirm Emergency Criteria
Senior clinical staff confirms and documents criteria are met.

### Step 2 — Override in OpesCare
Navigate to patient lookup → select "Emergency Access" → enter reason (min 20 chars).
System grants time-limited access (60 min). All data viewed is logged.

### Step 3 — Notification
OpesCare flags access in audit log. Compliance officer notified within 24 hours.
Patient notified as soon as clinically appropriate.

### Step 4 — Post-Emergency Review
Clinical lead reviews within 48 hours. Unjustified access escalated to governance.

## 4. Prohibitions
- Do NOT use for routine care
- Do NOT share data beyond immediate care team
- Do NOT browse unrelated record sections

## 5. Audit
All emergency access reviewed monthly by Compliance Officer.

# SOP-003 — Patient Consent

**SOP Number:** SOP-003 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Clinical Staff / Registration

---

## 1. Purpose
Ensure valid informed consent before accessing, sharing, or processing patient health data.

## 2. Types of Consent
- **Registration consent** — at first registration
- **Procedure consent** — before specific procedures
- **Data sharing consent** — before sharing with third parties
- **Research consent** — before any research use

## 3. Procedure

### Step 1 — Explain the Consent
Explain in patient's preferred language. Allow questions. Confirm understanding.

### Step 2 — Record in OpesCare
Navigate: Patient record → Consent section. Select type, record method (verbal/written/digital).

### Step 3 — Consent Refusal
Record refusal. Do NOT proceed. Inform clinical lead. Document in patient record.

## 4. Consent Revocation
Patient may revoke at any time via patient portal, in-person, or written request.
Revocation must be recorded same day.

# SOP-012 — Billing & Payments

**SOP Number:** SOP-012 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Billing Clerk / Cashier

---

## 1. Generate Invoice
Review auto-generated invoice. Verify all services captured. Confirm correct fee schedule.

## 2. Present to Patient
Show itemised invoice. Explain charges. Apply approved discounts (waiver code required).

## 3. Collect Payment
Accepted modes: Cash | POS/Card | Bank Transfer | Mobile Money | NHIS/HMO (SOP-013) | Wallet.
Record payment mode and amount in OpesCare.

## 4. Issue Receipt
Mark invoice paid. Print or email receipt.
Receipt includes: invoice number, services, amount, payment mode, cashier name.

## 5. Discounts & Waivers
All discounts require manager authorisation code and documentation.

## 6. Cash Handling
Count cash in front of patient. Issue receipt before patient leaves. Daily cash reconciliation.

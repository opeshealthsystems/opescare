# SOP-011 — Pharmacy Dispensing

**SOP Number:** SOP-011 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Pharmacist / Pharmacy Technician

---

## 1. Receive Prescription
Prescription appears in pharmacy queue. Verify authorisation and patient identity.

## 2. Check Stock
Confirm drug is available. If out of stock: notify clinician and offer approved substitute.

## 3. Dispense
1. Select stock item(s) matching prescription.
2. Record batch number and expiry.
3. Dispense quantity as prescribed.
4. Print label: patient name, Health ID, drug, dose instructions, dispensed date, expiry.

## 4. Counsel Patient
Explain dose, frequency, duration, side effects, drug-food interactions.
Advise patient not to share medication.

## 5. Complete in OpesCare
Mark prescription dispensed. Update inventory stock level. Record dispensing pharmacist.

## 6. Controlled Substances
Require pharmacist co-signature and controlled drug register entry.

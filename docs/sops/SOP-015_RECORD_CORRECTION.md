# SOP-015 — Record Correction

**SOP Number:** SOP-015 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Records Officer

---

## 1. Key Principle
OpesCare corrects by amendment, not deletion. Original records are preserved; corrections appended with timestamp and reason.

## 2. Procedure
1. Patient submits correction request (portal or in-person) specifying incorrect field(s) and correct information with evidence.
2. Records officer logs under Patient > Rights > Correction Request.
3. Review evidence within 30 days (NDPR requirement).
4. Consult clinical staff for clinical record corrections.
5. If approved: amend record, note amendment details, preserve original in audit trail.
6. If rejected: document reason, notify patient, allow statement of disagreement.
7. Notify patient of outcome within 30 days.

OpesCare Patient App — Design System Recommendation

Based on health industry standards (Epic MyChart, NHS App, Kaiser Permanente, WHO digital health guidelines) — Option B: Clean Light + Clinical Blue is the evidence-based choice.

Why Clean Light wins in healthcare:
  • WCAG AA contrast compliance — mandatory for medical apps (elderly patients, low vision)
  • Daylight readability — clinics, pharmacies, waiting rooms are brightly lit
  • Reduces cognitive load — white space helps patients parse critical health data
  • Industry standard: Epic MyChart, NHS App, Kaiser, WHO mHealth guidelines all use light themes
  • Dark navy primary (#1565C0) is proven "clinical trust" signal — not alarming, not casual
